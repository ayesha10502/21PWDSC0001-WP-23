DESTINATION GUIDES

SHIMLA HILLS

Places to Visit:

  • Shimla View Point
  • Hiking Trails
  • Pine Forests

Things To Do:

  • Take a nature walk
  • Photography
  • Enjoy a Picnic

Local Tips:

  • Wear Comfortable shoes for hiking
  • Visit in the morning for the best views
  • Respect the environment

HARNOI LAKE

Things To Do:

  • Boating and Kayaking
  • Picnicking by the Lakeside
  • Bird Watching
  • Nature Walk

Local Tips:

  • Visit during the morning for the best lighting and tranquility
  • Respect the natural environment and wildlife
  • Carry a picnic basket for a delightful lakeside meal
  • Don't forget your camera to capture the stunning landscapes

NATHIAGALI

Places to Visit:

  • Mukshpuri and Miranjani Trails
  • Nathiagali Church
  • Pine Top

Things to Do:

  • Hiking and trekking
  • Explore the church and cemetery
  • Enjoy local cuisine

Local Tips:

  • Carry warm clothing in the winter
  • Respect local culture and traditions
  • Support local businesses

DUNGA GALI

Places to Visit:

  • Pines Hotel
  • Ayubia National Park
  • Ayubia Chairlift

Things to Do:

  • Scenic walks and photography
  • Enjoy chairlift rides
  • Visit the local market

Local Tips:

  • Check the chairlift schedule
  • Try local snacks and street food
  • Respect the natural beauty of the area
